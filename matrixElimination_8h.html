Performs an elimination step by subtracting the pivot row from all the rows below with a non-zero co-efficient, to zero them out.

o_eliminationFactors is populated with the eliminated row indices and the corresponding factors.

Allowing the recording of the elimination factors at the respective rows allow the same operations to be efficiently replayed and performed on another matrix.
