Detailed Description

Matrix multiplication.

Vector normalization.

Mathematically described as the composition of two linear maps

The equation is formed as: A * B = AB.

Each entry (i, j) in the matrix product AB can be computed as the inner product of the i'th row of A and the j'th column of B.

Only applicable for matrices with a column count of 1 (a column vector).

Divide each component by its length.
